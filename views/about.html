What about us?

IndieWest started as a group of friends who wanted to make movies. Jenny, Albert, Kenny, and David met in high school in El Paso Texas. All of them valued the history of El Paso, and many other Western towns. They bonded over movies such as The Searchers, Stagecoach, and A Fistful of Dollars. All of them went on to attend the University of Texas. There they found a unique combination of courses and opportunities that facilitated the creation of IndieWest.
